Estructura correcta para un video TOP 10 / COUNTDOWN en VidRush
1. Title

Un título claro del tema.

Ejemplo:
Title: 10 Crazy Japanese Inventions That Should Exist Everywhere

2. Introducción (Hook + Overview)

Aquí van 2–3 talking points para abrir el video.

Estructura:

Hook: frase que genera curiosidad

Contexto rápido

Qué verá el espectador

Ejemplo:

Introduction:

Hook: Japan has inventions so smart that once you see them, you'll wonder why the rest of the world hasn't copied them.

These everyday innovations solve problems most countries still struggle with.

Today we're counting down 10 incredible Japanese inventions that should exist everywhere.

3. El Countdown (10 → 1)

Cada número debe tener:

idea principal

2-3 supporting facts

cosas visuales (para que VidRush encuentre footage)

Formato:

10. [Nombre del invento o cosa]

- Qué es
- Cómo funciona
- Por qué es sorprendente

Ejemplo:

10. Heated Sidewalks That Melt Snow

- Cities like Sapporo install heated pipes under sidewalks
- Snow melts automatically without shoveling or salt
- Keeps streets safe during heavy winter storms
- Used widely in northern Japan where snowfall is extreme

Luego:

9. Automatic Taxi Doors

- Taxi drivers control passenger doors with a lever
- Prevents people from slamming doors
- Standard across most Japanese taxi fleets
- First-time tourists are often shocked when the door opens automatically

Y así hasta el #1.

4. Número 1 (El más impactante)

Debe ser el más fuerte.

Formato:

1. [El mejor o más sorprendente]

- Revelación principal
- Impacto o por qué es increíble
- Algo visual fuerte

Ejemplo:

1. Silent Ambulance System

- Japanese ambulances use advanced traffic coordination
- Traffic lights turn green automatically for emergency vehicles
- Drivers receive alerts to move before the ambulance arrives
- Reduces response time dramatically in crowded cities
5. Conclusión

2–3 talking points.

Formato:

Conclusion:

- These inventions show how Japan designs everyday life with efficiency and respect
- Many of these ideas could easily improve cities around the world
- Which one do you think your country needs the most?
Regla importante para duración

Según VidRush:

Duración	Cantidad de puntos
8 min	4-5
10-12 min	7-8
12-15 min	Top 10 completo

Un Top 10 normalmente dura 12-15 minutos.
